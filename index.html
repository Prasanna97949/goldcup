Our Story

Founded in 1985 in Vallam, Thanjavur, GoldCup has been a pioneer in crafting refreshing beverages. From classic apple juice to traditional paneer soda,and many more. our journey has been one of passion and quality.

The Beginning

Goldcup was established in 1985 in Vallam, Thanjavur, with a dedication to crafting high-quality beverages. Starting as a small unit, they focused on perfecting traditional flavors with strict quality standards. Their soda and fruit-based drinks quickly gained popularity, leading to expansion beyond Thanjavur. Over the years, they introduced a diverse range of beverages, constantly innovating to meet customer preferences. Today, Goldcup remains a trusted name, known for its commitment to taste, purity, and excellence.

Crafting Flavors

Goldcup takes pride in crafting flavors that capture the essence of natural ingredients. Each beverage is carefully formulated to deliver a refreshing and authentic taste. From rich fruit juices to crisp sodas, their recipes balance tradition and innovation. They use high-quality ingredients to ensure consistency and satisfaction in every sip. With a passion for excellence, Goldcup continues to create flavors that delight generations.

A Legacy of Trust

Goldcup has built a legacy of trust since its inception in 1985. Their commitment to quality and authenticity has earned the loyalty of customers for decades. By maintaining strict production standards, they ensure every bottle meets expectations. Generations have enjoyed their beverages, making them a household name. With a strong foundation, Goldcup continues to uphold its reputation for excellence.
